## Button variant

Create an anchor with button styles by passing `variant="btn"` to an Anchor:

<Anchor variant='btn' color='primary' href='#'>
  Go Back
</Anchor>
<Anchor variant='btn' color='anchor' href='#'>

</Anchor>
