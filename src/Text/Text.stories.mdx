## Span text elements

Pass `inline` to render a `span` instead of a `p`:

<Text>
  This is a larger block of text that has a{' '}
  <Text fontWeight='bold' inline>
    bold
  </Text>{' '}
  text span inside of it.
</Text>

---

## Text sizes

Pass a size prop to change the font size:

<Text size='xl'>Example extra large text content.</Text>
<Text size='lg'>Example large text content.</Text>
<Text size='base'>Example text content.</Text>
<Text size='sm'>Example small text content.</Text>
<Text size='xs'>Example extra small text content.</Text>

---

## Lead

<Text lead>This is a lead paragraph that will stand out.</Text>
